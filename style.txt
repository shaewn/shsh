1) All types shall fall in one of three categories:
  a) keyword (e.g., int, unsigned, etc.)
  b) snake_case, ending with '_t' (e.g., uint64_t)
  c) PascalCase (e.g., CommandParser)
2) All variable names shall be snake_case.
3) All function names shall be snake_case.
4) All pointers go directly next to the variable names.
